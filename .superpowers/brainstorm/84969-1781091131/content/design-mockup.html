Design details

  • Component: IsometricCityChart.svelte — pure canvas, no deps
  • Height: total_cost_usd normalized to 80% of canvas height (max session = tallest)
  • Color: 4 amber/fire shades — baseline=amber, tools=orange, headroom=red, full=pink
  • Hover: highlight building + tooltip showing scenario, config, cost, duration, tokens in/out
  • Entrance: buildings animate up from ground on mount (300ms ease-out per building, staggered)
  • Click: clicking a building fires the existing drilldown (same as table row inspect)
  • Empty state: ghost outline placeholders when <3 sessions, "Run a sim to build the city" label
  • Placement: replaces the existing stat-cards row at the top of AnalyticsView — stat cards move inside the city card's footer bar
